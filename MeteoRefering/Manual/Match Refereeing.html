On the Subject of Match Refereeing

We're dealing with the lives of entire civilizations here, so let's be careful.

On this module, you can see a spaceship (named the Metamo Ark) as well as multiple planets.

Pressing the Metamo Ark will activate its sonar, which will make you hear how the Meteos match between these planets acted out.

You will hear the annihiliation theme of the planets that lost, from last place to first place. You will also hear the winning theme of the planet that is still standing. Note that you'll hear the themes of the first and second place planets at the same time.

HOWEVER, your sonar is rusty and has yet to be repaired, and may not function as intended. There is a possibility that you'll hear the outcome of a different match between other planets. Disregard it and use your sonar again.

Note that these other matches will have at least one planet not displayed on the module who participated.

Press a planet to annihiliate it. You must press them in the order they lost the match.

Pressing a planet that isn't supposed to be annihiliated at that time will make you responsible for the death of a whole civilization (You should feel bad). A strike will also occur.

Do that for 3 different matches, each having one more planet participating than the previous. Doing so will solve the module. You will not be held responsible for the death of those who lost.

The following pages will help you figure out which planet is which. You will find their name, look, winning and annihilation theme, as well as the name of the themes.
